The Guide Group Terms of Service for Clients

To save your time, here is a summary of the key points for quick understanding:

  • A 33% deposit is required online to reserve a guide.
  • The remaining 67% is paid directly to the guide on the tour day.
  • The deposit is generally non-refundable.
  • If the assigned guide cannot provide the service and no equivalent substitute can be arranged, the deposit will be refunded minus a 6% handling fee.
  • Clients must arrive 10 minutes early; arrivals over 30 minutes late are considered no-shows.
  • Tour time starts at the scheduled meeting time, regardless of arrival time.
  • Overtime and additional requests must be agreed upon in advance.
  • In cases of severe weather or official alerts, tours may be rescheduled or fully refunded.
  • The Group operates as a technical platform and coordination provider; all guide services are provided independently by Members.

Article 2 (Definitions)

4. The service contract for the tour is formed directly between the Client and the Member.
5. The Group does not employ, manage, supervise, or control Members and assumes no responsibility for the performance of guide services.

Article 3 (Reservations and Deposits)

1. A booking is confirmed only upon payment of a deposit equal to 33% of the total fee via the platform at the time of reservation.
2. The deposit constitutes a pre-payment for reservation and coordination services and is generally non-refundable.
3. The remaining 67% of the total fee shall be paid directly to the assigned Member on the tour day, in cash or via electronic payment methods accepted by the Member.

4. All transaction risks, including currency exchange or payment method issues, are borne by the Client and/or the Member.

Article 5 (Cancellations and Refunds)

1. The 33% deposit paid to confirm a reservation is generally non-refundable.
2. If the assigned Member is unable to provide the service and no equivalent substitute can be arranged, the deposit will be refunded minus a 6% handling fee.
3. Cancellation timing does not affect the refundability of the deposit.
4. Refunds, where applicable, will be processed through the original payment method, and any third-party fees shall be borne by the Client.
5. The Group provides coordination support but does not act as a dispute resolution authority.

Article 6 (Meeting Point and Punctuality)

1. Clients must arrive at least 10 minutes before the scheduled start time.
2. Tours depart within 30 minutes of the scheduled time. Arrivals exceeding 30 minutes are treated as no-shows without refund.
3. Tour time begins at the scheduled meeting time regardless of actual arrival.
