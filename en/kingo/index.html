My name is Kingo Iida, and I am a licensed tour guide based in Kyoto, Japan. I specialize in creating meaningful and personalized experiences in Kyoto and Nara, two of the most historic and culturally rich regions in the country. I have always been fascinated by Japanese history, spirituality, and traditional culture. Living near these ancient capitals has allowed me to study not only the famous temples and shrines, but also the deeper ideas behind them — such as Zen philosophy, samurai ethics, tea culture, and the concept of harmony with nature. I enjoy sharing these perspectives with visitors in a way that is engaging, thoughtful, and easy to understand. For me, travel is more than sightseeing. It is about connection — to people, to history, and sometimes even to oneself. When I guide guests, I try to create experiences that are both educational and personal. Whether we are walking through a quiet Zen temple, exploring a historic street in Kyoto, or visiting a sake brewery in Nara, I aim to explain not only what you see, but why it matters. I am also a certified sake professional, and I enjoy introducing guests to Japan’s rich fermentation culture. Sake is closely connected to rice farming, local climate, and craftsmanship. Through tastings and brewery visits, guests can discover another layer of Japanese tradition in a relaxed and enjoyable way. Some guests are interested in history and architecture, others in religion, martial arts, or food culture. I take time to understand each person’s interests and design an itinerary that matches their curiosity and pace. Privacy, comfort, and meaningful conversation are important parts of the experience I provide.

In addition to guiding, I continue to study English, global affairs, and cross-cultural communication so that I can better connect with international guests. I believe that cultural exchange should be a two-way dialogue. I enjoy learning from my guests just as much as I enjoy sharing Japan with them. Japan has a unique ability to balance tradition and modern life. My goal is to help visitors experience that balance in a genuine and memorable way. If you are looking not only to see Japan, but to understand it more deeply, I would be honored to guide you. Thank you and I look forward to seeing you soon.

In my free time, I enjoy traveling, ballet, and gardening. I take ballet lessons twice a week, which helps me maintain good physical strength and stamina.
Feel free to contact me by email at kingo@japanprivateguide.com or via video call on Guide Meet, in my room. I may not always be online, so please contact me by email first.

Before You Book

Please review the following important information before confirming your tour.

🌸 Cancellation & Refund Policy

  • A 35% deposit is required to secure your booking and is generally non-refundable.
  • If the guide is unable to conduct the tour and no replacement can be arranged, the deposit will be refunded minus a 6% administrative fee.
  • If you need to cancel or reschedule, please notify us as early as possible.

🌸 Meeting Point & Punctuality

  • Please arrive at the meeting point at least 10 minutes before the scheduled start time.
  • Tours will depart within 30 minutes of the scheduled start time.
  • Arriving more than 30 minutes late will be considered a no-show.

🌸 Payment & Guiding Service

  • A 35% deposit is required to confirm your reservation.
  • The remaining 65% balance must be paid directly to the guide on the day of the tour, either in cash or via accepted electronic payment methods.
  • Guiding services are provided independently, and the guide is responsible for delivering your tour experience.
  • TGG ensures secure payments and verifies guide identities, but does not directly manage the guiding service itself.

🌸 Tour Start Time & Billing

  • The tour officially begins at the scheduled meeting time, not at the participant’s arrival time.
  • Late arrivals do not affect the duration of the tour or the billing.

🌸 Overtime & Additional Charges

  • Overtime will be charged based on the “Overtime Add-On” specified in your booking.
  • Any request to extend the tour must be confirmed with the guide in advance.

🌸 What’s Included

  • The price of the tour covers guide services and coordination support.
  • Transportation, meals, and entrance fees to attractions are not included unless explicitly stated in the package.

🌸 Weather Conditions & Emergencies

  • Tours will proceed in light to moderate rain.
  • If severe weather (such as typhoons or storms) occurs, the tour may be rescheduled or fully refunded.

🌸 Invoice & Receipt

  • For invoicing of guiding services, please contact the guide directly.
  • TGG can assist with coordination if needed, but does not issue invoices on behalf of guides.
