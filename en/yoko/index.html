Before You Book

Please review the following important information before confirming your tour.

🌸 Cancellation & Refund Policy

  • A 35% deposit is required to secure your booking and is generally non-refundable.
  • If the guide is unable to conduct the tour and no replacement can be arranged, the deposit will be refunded minus a 6% administrative fee.
  • If you need to cancel or reschedule, please notify us as early as possible.

🌸 Meeting Point & Punctuality

  • Please arrive at the meeting point at least 10 minutes before the scheduled start time.
  • Tours will depart within 30 minutes of the scheduled start time.
  • Arriving more than 30 minutes late will be considered a no-show.

🌸 Payment & Guiding Service

  • A 35% deposit is required to confirm your reservation.
  • The remaining 65% balance must be paid directly to the guide on the day of the tour, either in cash or via accepted electronic payment methods.
  • Guiding services are provided independently, and the guide is responsible for delivering your tour experience.
  • TGG ensures secure payments and verifies guide identities, but does not directly manage the guiding service itself.

🌸 Tour Start Time & Billing

  • The tour officially begins at the scheduled meeting time, not at the participant’s arrival time.
  • Late arrivals do not affect the duration of the tour or the billing.

🌸 Overtime & Additional Charges

  • Overtime will be charged based on the “Overtime Add-On” specified in your booking.
  • Any request to extend the tour must be confirmed with the guide in advance.

🌸 What’s Included

  • The price of the tour covers guide services and coordination support.
  • Transportation, meals, and entrance fees to attractions are not included unless explicitly stated in the package.

🌸 Weather Conditions & Emergencies

  • Tours will proceed in light to moderate rain.
  • If severe weather (such as typhoons or storms) occurs, the tour may be rescheduled or fully refunded.

🌸 Invoice & Receipt

  • For invoicing of guiding services, please contact the guide directly.
  • TGG can assist with coordination if needed, but does not issue invoices on behalf of guides.
