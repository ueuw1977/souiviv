Trusted online support for foreigners living in Japan. The Sakura Team is a comprehensive online support service made up of 22–26 experienced support staff members, including external specialists. We provide clear, multilingual guidance to help foreign residents and visitors navigate daily life in Japan. Our support covers a wide range of areas, including online interpretation and translation, Japanese conversation practice, daily administrative procedures, job and school consultations, visa application support, medical and social insurance information, tax-related guidance, and travel support.

To date, we have supported more than 1,000 foreign residents, from those who have just arrived in Japan to those who have lived here for many years. We are highly trusted and often described as “reliable,” “reassuring,” and “easy to understand.” Our team values kindness, patience, and careful communication, and we take every question and concern seriously—no matter how small—while offering practical and appropriate guidance.

All services are provided online using the Sodan Center online meeting tool, allowing us to offer immediate support from anywhere in Japan. For travelers who have not yet arrived, we also provide advance guidance and support to help ensure a smooth and comfortable stay in Japan.

We provide general guidance and information support for daily life in Japan. Please note that our services do not include licensed legal, medical, or tax services.

If you have any concerns about living in Japan or handling procedures, please feel free to contact us. The Sakura Team is here to help turn your worries into peace of mind.

If you have any questions before making a reservation, please don’t hesitate to contact us via Live Chat, Online Consultation or foreign-support@sodancenter.com.

Before You Book

Please review the following important points before booking.

Cancellation & Refund Policy

  • Cancellation more than 12 hours before the reserved start time: No cancellation fee applies. Any unused hours can be carried over for your next session.
    *Hours cancelled before 12 hours can be carried over to future use.
  • Cancellation within 12 hours of the reserved start time: If you cancel or fail to show up without notice, reserved hours cannot be carried over.
    *For example, if you booked a 2-hour session, those 2 hours cannot be carried over.
    *Hours already used cannot be carried over.
  • Cancellation or interruption after service start: Once the service has started, any cancellation or interruption will forfeit all reserved hours, including unused portions.
    *The session will be counted from the reserved hours; please note that stopping mid-session will not reduce charges.
  • Refund handling fee: If a refund is issued, processing fees charged by the payment provider (e.g., 6%) will be deducted from the refunded amount.

Payment

  • Please purchase the required time package before the session starts and pay online via PayPal, PayPay, credit card, bank transfer, etc.
  • Payments must be made in advance; the service will start once payment is confirmed.
  • Time packages can be used across all services (e.g., 30 min, 1 hour, or multiple hours).
  • Unused hours can be carried over to future sessions.
  • Any currency exchange or payment processing fees are the responsibility of the customer.

Service Time & Fees

  • Reserved time packages can be applied to any service, but the minimum booking per session is 30 minutes.
  • Fees are calculated from the earlier of the reserved start time or online connection established.
  • Delays or unpreparedness on the customer’s side cannot adjust session time or fees.
  • If the customer leaves or loses connection during a session, that time is counted as service time, including waiting time.
  • Even if the service is interrupted or stopped mid-session, fees for the reserved time still apply.

Service Extension

  • If the session exceeds the reserved time, additional time will be billed based on standard rates. Extensions must be agreed upon with staff during the session.
  • Extension is subject to discussion with the staff during the online service.
  • Time calculation follows these increments:
    ・0–30 min: 30-min rate
    ・31–60 min: 60-min rate
    ・61–90 min: 90-min rate
    ・After 90 min, additional 30-min increments apply.

What’s Included

  • Fees cover basic support required for online services. Please note that we do not provide on-site services.
  • All services are provided online through the Sodan Center consultation room and live chat. We do not offer services via phone, LINE, WhatsApp, or other communication tools outside Sodan Center.

Staff Assignment

  • Staff is not fixed per session. If the originally assigned staff cannot attend, another qualified staff member will provide the service. Staff assignments may change depending on circumstances.

Emergencies & Force Majeure

  • Internet or communication issues may cause service delays. We appreciate your understanding.
  • In case of unavoidable delays or cancellations due to natural disasters or emergencies, alternative scheduling will be arranged.

Invoicing

  • Invoices can be issued for corporate clients upon request.
  • For any questions regarding invoices, please contact the service team.
